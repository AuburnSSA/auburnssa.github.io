ENGLISH We Create Designs and technology

Founded in April 2018, AUSSA is committed to serving Auburn students from all aspects of life, entertainment, academics, and professional development as a non-political, non-religious, non-profit autonomous student and scholar groups.

Most of the member of AUSSA are Chinese, at the same time, Chinese are our main service group. We hope student can get developed and make friends with each other within our team!

Work hard, Play hard

We have already built up a online community for Chiense Student living in Auburn. From bbs to Wechat Mini Program, we are always trying to providing best service to the students.

For now, we have two WeChat Mini Program are accessed to more than 2500 Chinese student in Auburn. One is a local bbs app and another one is a carpool app for people live in Auburn.

A better environment for work and live in Auburn

Building an culture birdge between Chinese and other students living in Auburn.

Bring our service to more students!
